WHAT IS TRANSCREATION?

Transcreation is a word we hear quite often these days, but maybe the term "localization copywriting" is a little more self-explanatory. Call it what you like, it is the process of not just translating a piece of text but rewriting it in another language. The linguistic devices and imagery might be significantly adapted to the target language and culture, but the effect on the reader remains the same. It is definitely not just about the words.
