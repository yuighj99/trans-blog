WHAT IS TRANSCREATION?

Transcreation is a word we hear quite often these days, but maybe the term "localization copywriting" is a little more self-explanatory. Call it what you like, it is the process of not just translating text but rewriting a message in another language and, where necessary, adapting it culturally.
